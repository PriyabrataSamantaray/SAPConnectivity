RDE Resource Management Frequently Asked Questions

 

Who has access to this application?

Access to the project planning functionality is restricted to project leaders of active R&D projects and points of contact, which the project leader chooses to collaborate with for planning their specific project. Access to the resource assignment functionality is restricted to first-level managers of employees who work on R&D projects.

 

I am a Project Leader of a R&D project but am not able to access the application. How do I get help?

There may be 2 reasons why you are not able to access the application. If you are working on an exploratory or released product management R&D project, your BU may have chosen to plan for such project under "a single bucket" internal order. Please consult with your BU Ops to find out if your project has a distinct internal order in SAP or is part of a "bucket" internal order in SAP.

If your project has a distinct internal order in SAP and you don't have access to this application, this is due to a different person listed as the project leader in the SAP internal order. Please consult with your BU Ops to make sure that you are listed as the "User Responsible" in SAP for the internal order of your R&D project.

 

I am a first-level manager of employees who work on R&D projects but am not able to access the application. How do I get help?

Please submit a Help Desk ticket.

 

I am working on R&D project and want to see its resource plan. How do I get help?

Please consult with the project leader of your project to get a copy of the resources plan (labor and controllable). If you are part of CSS, CSG or NPI, please talk with your project leader who can grant you access to their project in a collaborative planning role for CSS, CSG or NPI resources respectively.
